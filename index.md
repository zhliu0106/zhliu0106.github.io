Hi, my name is **Zhenhua Liu** (**刘振华**).
I am a Ph.D. student at Soochow University, advised by [Prof. Wenliang Chen](https://chen-wenliang.github.io/). I received my B.S. degree from Shandong University.

My main research interest focuses on Trustworthy AI and Alignment. Before that, I worked on Open-domain Dialogue Systems.
